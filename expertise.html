Our Team

Industrial.

The Industrial sector presents both great opportunities and significant challenges for all players in this arena, calling for leaders with insight and a strong ability to implement strategies for results. With increased public and political scrutiny around sustainability and eco-friendly production and distribution, our clients in the Industrial sector seek highly qualified talents to meet all the challenges while seizing opportunities for robust growth.

Our consultants strive to identify and assess top executives and senior functional leadership, as well as conduct in-depth cultural assessment and cultural-fit analyses for our clients in the Industrial sector:

We have a focused team on the chemical industry, with capability spanning the entire chemical product manufacturing chain, from upstream through downstream, covering petroleum, natural gas and coal, basic chemicals and fine chemicals, and all the way to downstream chemical companies in agrochemical, coating, plastics, fiber, synthetic materials and other fields.

We recruit talents for clients in machinery industry with processes such as mechanical, electro-mechanical, CNC machining and assembly, as well as the manufacturing of finished goods in machinery parts and components and complete machineries such as agro/ farm equipment, construction equipment, mining equipment, and other engineered products.

We help our clients in energy/ new energy/ renewable energy/ clean energy field to identify all kinds of leaders who can address sustainability and geopolitical risk, improve performance and operational efficiency while navigating the complex regulatory environment.

We have years of experience helping our clients in automotive sector, identifying talents in an extremely competition-intensive market. With China being the largest motor vehicle producing country, numerous players here in this sector specialize in automotive engines, automotive electronics, seat control systems, and automotive peripherals, with a strong demand for top talents who can make a difference.

We also work with clients in aerospace and defense sector, both for components makers and equipment assemblers, in identifying and evaluating talents who can meet their requirements and standards.
